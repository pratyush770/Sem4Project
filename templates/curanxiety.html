Remedies for Anxiety Disorder

Consistent feelings of anxiety can prove to be unhealthy and even start to affect the quality of your life and prevent you from working efficiently. However,
fortunately you can deal with it. Some methods are given below.

1) Practice Mindfulness


Mindfulness is a way of living that helps you face your fear and live life fully, irrespective of what the future hold for you.
Mindfulness is just the exact opposite of anxiety. People with anxiety can't stop worrying. Whereas with mindfulness,
you will be able to improve focus in life and reduce anxiety without the need for any medication.

2) Keep Yourself Physically Active


Physical exercise is one of the best ways of reducing stress and anxiety. It acts like a mood booster and eases depression
and anxiety by releasing endorphins which are the chemicals in the brain that act as natural painkillers and reduces stress.
Exercise may improve mental health by helping the brain cope better with stress. In one study, researchers found that
those who got regular vigorous exercise were 25 percent less likely to develop anxiety disorder over the next 5 years.

3) Avoid Triggers (Alcohol, Caffeine, etc.)


Excessive consumption of alcohol and caffeine can make it even more difficult to cope with your anxiety and stress,
worsening the situation, consuming alcohol in high amounts might lead to greater alocohol dependence, blackout, brain
damage, etc. which will only increase your anxiety and stress. Excessive caffeine consumption can lead to symptoms
similar to psychiatric conditions including sleep and anxiety disorders, anxiety, and psychotic symptoms.
